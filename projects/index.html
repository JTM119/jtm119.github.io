Department of Energy (DOE)

GRIDview

Uncertainty-aware grid decision support platform for probabilistic forecasting, risk visualization, and simulation-based operating decisions. Benchmarked Gaussian Processes, Bayesian Neural Networks, and TCNs with Student-t likelihood heads; the TCN achieved 27.1% CRPS improvement over a deterministic baseline with calibrated 90%, 95%, and 99% prediction interval coverage. Used Gaussian copula scenario generation to preserve cross-variable dependencies across load, solar, and wind forecasts. Deployed via FastAPI, PyTorch, React, D3.js, Docker, and Azure DevOps CI/CD with sub-one-minute end-to-end latency.

  • Gaussian Processes

EmiFor

DOE amine emissions forecasting and decision-support framework for carbon capture operations. Developed forecasting models using TCN baselines, quantile forecasting, Neural Differential Equations, and physics-informed step-response models. Built a synthetic data pipeline using vine-copula residual dependencies to augment sparse experimental data. Best MAPE of 4.2% on synthetic data and 4.4% on Niederaussem experimental data. Delivered causal impact analysis, counterfactual evaluation, and a Streamlit application.

ScopeVis

DOE toolkit for AI-assisted visualization of nanoscale microscopy data, developed in partnership with Brookhaven National Laboratory. Implemented and evaluated five segmentation approaches — SAM, MicroSAM, 3D watershed, region growing, and a custom Sobel-edge watershed — plus graph-based clustering to join 2D masks into 3D objects. Used entropy-based threshold selection, KDE background estimation, mutual-information scoring, and TPE Bayesian optimization. Delivered all five technical objectives at 100% completion, demonstrating an estimated 94% time reduction over manual methods.

Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)

Pelarion

Full-stack systematic review platform built for the EPA to accelerate grey literature screening. Ingests PDFs and HTML, segments documents into sentence-aware chunks, and ranks abstracts with a two-stage pipeline: BM25 lexical ranking followed by a Random Forest / XGBoost classifier tuned via Optuna. Full-text screening uses an LLM (Gemini Flash) with structured prompts, producing criterion-by-criterion rationales and page-level citations. Delivered 80% recall and 86% precision. Deployed as a production web app with Django, React, PostgreSQL, Celery, RabbitMQ, Redis, and Azure Blob Storage.

  • PostgreSQL
  • LLMs
  • XGBoost

Department of Defense (DoD)

AILA

Multimodal AI prototype for the DoD that prioritizes targeting-package review by detecting mismatches between imagery and captions. Fine-tuned BLIP on a 1,435-pair dataset of overhead and satellite imagery across four target categories. Developed milBERT via the ExBERT architecture to improve masked language modeling on military text, raising top-1 accuracy from 0.394 to 0.498. Final model: 81.9% accuracy, 87.2% recall, 79.2% precision, with simulated 4x+ reviewer speedup. Delivered saliency maps and LIME explanations; deployed via React, Django, Flask, PostgreSQL, Kubernetes, and NGINX.

U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA)

Silva

Explainable GIS-based ecological risk assessment tool for forestry operations, built under USDA STTR Phase I. Encoded expert knowledge into a Bayesian Belief Network spanning 50+ environmental variables and 14 best management practices to generate synthetic training data. Trained XGBoost and GNN baselines on that synthetic data, achieving ECE under 6% — prioritizing calibrated risk probabilities over binary classification. Delivered as an offline-capable Progressive Web App with interactive subregion mapping, auto-populated soil and hydrology data, and BMP recommendations with per-recommendation risk impact estimates. Conducted 25 stakeholder interviews.

Demeter

Data-driven agricultural safety platform built under USDA Phase II SBIR. Incorporated NLP embeddings (ExBERT fine-tuned on AgInjuryNews) to encode free-text hazard descriptions, enabling a Graph Neural Network to generalize risk patterns to previously unseen hazards across diverse farm operations. Added a CLIP-based image-text verification module to validate photo evidence in safety records. GNN improvements — Laplacian positional encodings, refined attention-convolution layering, Optuna/MLflow hyperparameter tuning — raised accuracy from 94.9% to 96.3% and recall from 83.3% to 86.8%. Delivered as an offline-capable PWA with a 5-step observation workflow completable in under 60 seconds.

UAS Certification

NASA Phase I SBIR developing a Verification and Validation (V&V) Testing Environment for autonomous UAS certification, in partnership with the University of Cincinnati. Built on Microsoft AirSim and Unreal Engine 4, the TE injects sensor and hardware failure scenarios — GPS corruption, ML input corruption via Gaussian blur and pixel obscuration — and evaluates UAS responses using Monte Carlo trials. Demonstrated GPS failure detection across 80 simulated flights per path-planning algorithm, and confirmed TE failure detection matches independent ML model predictions across five camera corruption conditions.
